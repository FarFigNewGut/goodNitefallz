What it does: For the selected race and profession, the program calculates the initial stat placement that yields the highest stat total at level 100. Training points are not a consideration. Note that for any profession/race combination, there are multiple placements that yield the same total stats at level 100, so adjusting the Minimum Stats at Level 100 is recommended to find the best level 100 stats for your goals.

Race and Profession: Self-explanatory.

Minimum Stats at Level 100: These are the minimum acceptable values for each stat when the character reaches level 100. If you enter 95 for CO, for instance, the program will not place an initial value in CO lower than that which grows to 95 by level 100.

Reset Button: Clears the text fields.

Calculate Button: Begins the calculation. The results are displayed on a dynamically generated web page.

Browser Notes: This program works in at least Internet Explorer 5.5 and above and in Netscape Navigator 7.0. It does not work in Netscape 4.7 and lower, because they do not support the list boxes used.
